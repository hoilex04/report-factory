> [!abstract] Core Insight
> Qianxun Intelligence releases Spirit v1.5, the first Chinese open-source VLA model exceeding Physical Intelligence's π0.5 on Flow Matching architecture. Achieves SOTA in zero-shot cross-embodiment generalization, marking Chinese teams entering VLA first tier.

> [!note] Quantitative Data
> - **Inference Latency**: 150ms (vs π0.5's 180ms, 17% improvement)
> - **Zero-shot Success Rate**: 50% cross-embodiment (LIBERO benchmark)
> - **Training Efficiency**: 5× improvement (FAST frequency transform)
> - **Data Scale**: 200,000 hours UMI data pre-training
> - **Action Compression**: 27 tokens per 0.8s action block, 98% fidelity

> [!success] Key Breakthroughs
> 1. **Flow Matching Optimization**: 4-10 step generation vs diffusion's 16-50 steps
> 2. **RVQ Action Representation**: Hierarchical codebook for action tokenization
> 3. **Frequency Transform + BPE**: Training speedup through spectral domain processing
> 4. **Cross-Embodiment Pre-training**: Multi-robot data alignment strategy

> [!info] Technical Highlights
> - Architecture: Transformer-based VLA with Flow Matching head
> - Input: RGB images + language instruction
> - Output: Tokenized action sequences
> - Training: 200K hours UMI + 10K hours robot data
