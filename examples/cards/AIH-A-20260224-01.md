> [!abstract] Core Argument
> H1 2026 shows three convergence signals for edge AI: 1-bit quantization becomes practical, consumer GPUs can run 7B models, and inference cost drops to 1/10 of cloud. Edge VLA transitions from "lab toy" to "field productivity".

> [!tip] Key Insights
> - **Quantization Breakthrough**: HBVLA achieves 1-bit post-training quantization with memory reduced to 30% and <5% performance loss
> - **Deployment Simplicity**: StarSea G0 enables 5-minute deployment with 250M params and 10Hz inference on single GPU
> - **Cost Inflection**: RTX 5090 single-card VLA deployment at 1/10 the cost of cloud alternatives
